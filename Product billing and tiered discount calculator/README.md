\# Product Billing and Tiered Discount Calculator



\## Description

A Python script that takes a product's name, unit price, and quantity to calculate a total bill. It automatically evaluates the order size and applies a tiered discount before outputting the final price.



\## Features \& Rules

\* \*\*Bulk Discount (20% off):\*\* Automatically applied when purchasing 20 or more items.

\* \*\*Partial Discount (10% off):\*\* Applied for medium orders of 15 to 19 items.

\* \*\*No Discount (0% off):\*\* Standard pricing for orders under 15 items.

\* \*\*Final Price Breakdown:\*\* Displays the exact discount amount deducted and the final net total.
